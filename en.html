About

Venerdì House is a holiday home located in the heart of Alghero, ideal for families and couples. A few minutes from the old town and a short walk from San Giovanni Beach. A place where you can feel at home.

Our apartment

Venerdì House is a spacious 80 sqm apartment on the second floor without a lift. Two bedrooms, living room with fully equipped kitchen, bathroom and balconies. Air conditioning, free Wi‑Fi and all the comforts for a pleasant stay in Alghero.
