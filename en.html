Your holiday apartment in Alghero, near the beach and the historic centre

About

Venerdì House is a holiday apartment in Alghero designed for families and couples who want to stay in a convenient area, just a few minutes from the historic centre and close to San Giovanni Beach.

The apartment offers welcoming, functional spaces for enjoying Sardinia at your own pace, with easy access to the sea, the town centre and everyday services.

By booking directly on our official website, you can enjoy direct contact and reserved rates.

Our apartment

Venerdì House is an 80 sqm holiday apartment on the second floor with a lift, ideal for anyone looking for a comfortable and relaxing stay in Alghero.

The apartment includes two bedrooms, a living room, a fully equipped kitchen, a bathroom and balconies, with spaces designed to welcome both couples and families.

Air conditioning, free Wi-Fi and the location close to the beach and the historic centre make Venerdì House a practical and pleasant choice for enjoying Alghero with complete freedom.

Our rooms

The rooms at Venerdì House are designed to offer comfort, peace and flexibility throughout your stay.

The main double bedroom is spacious and bright, ideal for anyone looking for a welcoming and relaxing space after a day by the sea or in town.

The second bedroom has twin beds that can be joined into a double, a practical solution for couples, families or small groups.

How to find us

Venerdì House is located at Via Pasquale Paoli, 103 - 07041 Alghero SS, in one of the most convenient areas of Alghero, between the beach, the town centre and local services.

The apartment is about 100 metres from Lido and San Giovanni beaches and about 700 metres from Alghero’s historic centre, making it easy to enjoy your holiday on foot.

Bus stops, supermarkets, bars and restaurants are also close by, making your stay even more practical and enjoyable.
