Rally Italia Sardegna 2026

The WRC returns to Alghero

Dates of Rally Italia Sardegna 2026

Rally Italia Sardegna 2026 will take place in Alghero from 1 to 4 October 2026. It will be one of the most eagerly awaited sporting events of the autumn in Sardinia, bringing rally fans, teams and visitors to the city.

The WRC returns to Alghero

Rally Italia Sardegna is one of the most spectacular rounds of the FIA World Rally Championship. In 2026, Alghero will once again host the centre of the event, confirming its role as one of the symbolic homes of rallying in Sardinia.

The gravel roads, the landscapes of the Riviera del Corallo and the international atmosphere of the world championship make this event especially fascinating, even for those experiencing it for the first time.

An important round of the championship

The 2026 edition will be the penultimate round of the WRC World Championship and also the last European event of the season. This makes Rally Italia Sardegna even more interesting for motorsport fans, because it could play a decisive role in the outcome of the championship.

Why visit Alghero during the rally?

Staying in Alghero during the rally means experiencing a special atmosphere that combines sport, engines and holiday time. During the days of the event, the city becomes lively and vibrant, while still remaining a wonderful place for walks along the seafront, visits to the historic centre and moments of relaxation by the sea surrounded by the landscapes of Sardinia.

Where to stay in Alghero during the WRC

Venerdì House is a holiday home in Alghero, ideal for those who want to stay in the city during Rally Italia Sardegna. It is a convenient solution for enjoying the event while also discovering Alghero during one of the most atmospheric times of the year.
