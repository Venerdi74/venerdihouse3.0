Rally Italia Sardegna 2026 in Alghero

The WRC returns to Alghero from 1 to 4 October

Rally Italia Sardegna returns to Alghero

If you are looking for information about Rally Italia Sardegna 2026 in Alghero, this guide will help you understand when the event takes place, why it matters in the WRC calendar and why staying in town can be one of the most convenient ways to experience it.

During rally days, Alghero welcomes fans, teams and visitors from many countries. The city becomes one of the key points of the event and offers a special atmosphere, combining motorsport, movement and the chance to experience one of the most anticipated rounds of the championship from up close.

Dates of Rally Italia Sardegna 2026

Rally Italia Sardegna 2026 will take place in Alghero from 1 to 4 October 2026. It will be one of the most anticipated sporting events of the autumn season in Sardinia, bringing rally fans, teams and international visitors to the city.

For motorsport enthusiasts, this edition will be especially important. The 2026 rally will be the penultimate round of the WRC season and also the final European event of the championship, which means the Alghero weekend could play a decisive role in the title battle.

Why the rally in Alghero is special

Rally Italia Sardegna is one of the most spectacular rounds of the FIA World Rally Championship because it combines high-level competition with a unique natural setting. The gravel roads, the light of Sardinia and the panoramic stretches of the Riviera del Corallo make this event especially memorable, even for first-time visitors.

In Alghero, the rally is not just a sporting event taking place nearby. It becomes part of the city itself. Between the seafront, the old town, local services and the atmosphere of the race weekend, staying here allows you to combine your passion for the WRC with the pleasure of discovering Alghero at one of the liveliest times of the year.

Where to stay in Alghero during the WRC

Staying directly in Alghero during Rally Italia Sardegna is the most practical choice for visitors who want to enjoy the event more comfortably. Being based in town means you can follow the rally while also having restaurants, the historic centre, the seafront and the main local services within easy reach.

Venerdì House is a holiday home in Alghero that is ideal for guests who want to attend the rally while staying in a convenient area, close to the sea and well connected to the city. It is a great option for couples and families who want to combine the event with a few relaxing days in Sardinia.

During the rally weekend, accommodation in Alghero can become more in demand, so booking in advance is recommended, especially if you are looking for a comfortable place to enjoy the WRC without stress.
