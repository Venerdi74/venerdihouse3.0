Rally Italia Sardegna returns to Alghero

If you are looking for information about Rally Italia Sardegna 2026 in Alghero, this guide will help you understand why the event matters, when it takes place and why staying in Alghero is one of the best ways to experience it.

Rally Italia Sardegna is one of the most exciting rounds of the FIA World Rally Championship, and in 2026 Alghero will once again host the event base. During rally days, the city takes on a very special atmosphere: there is movement, energy and a strong international feel, but at the same time Alghero remains a beautiful place to enjoy the sea, the old town and the Sardinian coastline.

Dates of Rally Italia Sardegna 2026

Rally Italia Sardegna 2026 will take place in Alghero from 1 to 4 October 2026. It will be one of the most anticipated sporting events of the autumn season in Sardinia, bringing rally fans, teams and visitors to the city.

For motorsport enthusiasts, this will be an especially important edition. The 2026 rally will be the penultimate round of the WRC season and also the final European event of the championship, which means Alghero could host a key moment in the title fight.

Why the rally in Alghero is special

What makes this event so memorable is the combination of world-class competition and extraordinary scenery. The gravel roads, the light of Sardinia, the views of the Riviera del Corallo and the overall atmosphere of the world championship create something that is impressive even for first-time visitors.

In Alghero, the rally is not just something happening outside the city: it becomes part of the rhythm of the town itself. Walking along the seafront or through the historic centre during those days feels different, because you can sense that one of the most important sporting events in Sardinia is taking place around you.

Where to stay in Alghero during the WRC

Staying in Alghero during Rally Italia Sardegna is the most practical option for anyone who wants to enjoy the event without unnecessary travel. Being based in the city means you can experience the atmosphere more fully and at the same time enjoy restaurants, walks, the old town and the seafront.

Venerdì House is a holiday home in Alghero designed for guests who want to stay comfortably in town during the rally. It is an ideal choice for those who want to combine the excitement of the WRC with the pleasure of discovering Alghero in one of the most evocative moments of the year.

That is one of the biggest advantages of this event: it is not only about motorsport, but also about experiencing Alghero from a different perspective, with the city feeling lively and full of energy while still remaining a wonderful holiday destination.
