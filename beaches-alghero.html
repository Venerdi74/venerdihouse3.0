The most beautiful beaches in Alghero

A useful guide to help you plan your beach holiday

Alghero and its sea

Alghero is one of the most loved destinations in Sardinia thanks to its beaches, clear sea and the beautiful views of the Riviera del Corallo. By staying at Venerdì House, you can easily reach the coast and enjoy relaxing days among sandy beaches, small coves and beautiful natural spots.

If you are looking for a holiday home in Alghero near the sea, knowing the most beautiful beaches in the area can help you choose the best way to enjoy your holiday.

Lido di Alghero

Lido di Alghero is one of the most convenient beaches for those staying in town. It is ideal for families, couples and anyone looking for an easy-to-reach beach without long journeys. The sand is light, the seabed is quite shallow and the location is perfect for those who want to combine time at the beach with walks along the seafront.

Maria Pia

Maria Pia is one of the most popular beaches in Alghero. It is known for its fine sand, clear water and the beautiful pine forest that offers shade during the hottest hours of the day. It is an excellent choice for those looking for a wide, bright beach that is ideal for spending many relaxing hours by the sea.

Le Bombarde

Le Bombarde is one of the most famous beaches on the Alghero coast. The sea here is particularly clear and the colours are among the most beautiful in the area. It is highly appreciated by those who want to spend a day in a lively setting, with one of the most spectacular beaches of the Riviera del Corallo.

Il Lazzaretto

Not far from Le Bombarde lies Il Lazzaretto, an area much appreciated for its landscape and small coves. It is a perfect destination for those looking for crystal-clear water and a slightly more intimate atmosphere compared with the larger beaches.

Mugoni

The beach of Mugoni is ideal for those who love the sea surrounded by nature. The gulf offers a very relaxing setting and a pleasant landscape, especially in the late afternoon and at sunset. It is one of the best beaches for those seeking tranquillity and wide open spaces.

Porto Ferro

Porto Ferro is a wilder and more natural beach, loved by those who prefer more open landscapes and less urban surroundings. It is perfect for anyone who wants to discover a different side of the Alghero coast and enjoy a day by the sea in a more authentic setting.

Which beach should you choose in Alghero?

If you are looking for comfort and proximity to the town centre, Lido and Maria Pia are among the best options. If, on the other hand, you want spectacular sea views and intense colours, Le Bombarde and Lazzaretto are among the most famous choices. For a quieter and more natural atmosphere, Mugoni and Porto Ferro are two excellent alternatives.
