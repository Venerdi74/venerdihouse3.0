Data is processed using electronic and paper tools, according to the principles of lawfulness, fairness, transparency and minimization, with appropriate security measures.

Data may be processed by providers that supply technical/organizational services (hosting, maintenance, form handling). In the event of transfers outside the EU, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) or other appropriate safeguards under Arts. 44 et seq. GDPR are applied.

5. Retention periods

  • Cookie consent: up to 6 months (then consent is requested again).

This site uses necessary technical cookies. Third‑party services (e.g., Google Maps) are activated only after your consent via the banner or the dedicated button in the map section.
